FAQ

How does the app work?

To add a new workout or meal, simply click the '+' button located at the top of the Overview, Meal, or Workout tabs. This action will open a form for you to fill out with the relevant details. Once the form is submitted, the app will automatically generate workouts and meals for each day you've specified in the form.

How do I use the checklist?

To navigate the checklist, click on the numbers displayed at the top, representing the number of days you entered in the form during workout or meal planning. Clicking these numbers will update the checklist, reflecting your progress. The accompanying chart will dynamically adjust to showcase your achievements and align with your set goals.

Is the app suitable for specific dietary restrictions or preferences?

Yes, the app currently offers meal plans tailored for vegetarian and vegan preferences, along with customizable caloric goals. In the future, we plan on expanding our offerings to include a broader range of dietary plans and preferences in the future.

How are my meals and workouts generated?

Our app generates meals and workouts from a comprehensive database that includes preset meals and workout plans. These meals are carefully selected to offer a diverse range and help you meet your caloric goals. Our workouts have been meticulously crafted and tested by experienced coaches and individuals to provide effective and well-rounded fitness routines.
